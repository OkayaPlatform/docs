Okaya provides developers with a comprehensive infrastructure to efficiently manage and optimize AI prompts within a unified web platform.

Our platform empowers users to take full control of their prompts, allowing them to:

Choose the AI models that best suit their needs.
Determine the most effective questions (prompts) for their applications.
Monitor and manage their prompt-related expenditures.
Discover correlations between different prompts.
Access a complete version history of their prompts.
With Okaya, you can streamline your prompt management process, making it accessible to both software engineers and non-technical project managers.

Explore our comprehensive documentation to learn how to monitor, observe, and optimize your prompts using the Okaya platform.

Stay updated with the latest news and developments by following us on Twitter and joining our Discord community."
